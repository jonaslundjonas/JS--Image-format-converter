How it works & Supported Formats expand_more

Easy Image Converter runs entirely in your web browser. Your images are processed locally using Canvas APIs and modern decoding libraries. Your files are never uploaded to any server, keeping your photos completely private and secure.

Supported Input Formats:
  • PNG (.png)
  • JPEG / JPG (.jpg, .jpeg)
  • WebP (.webp)
  • SVG (.svg)
  • TIFF (.tiff, .tif)
  • HEIC / HEIF (.heic, .heif)
  • GIF (.gif)
  • BMP (.bmp)
Supported Output Formats:
  • PNG (Lossless, alpha)
  • JPEG (Solid background)
  • WebP (Compressed)
  • TIFF (Professional Lossless)
  • BMP (Standard Bitmap)
  • ICO (Favicon / Icon)
  • GIF (Static Image)
